The problem

Public trust in policing is collapsing. Police misconduct is rampant and there is a lack of resources, staff and the technology needed to combat this major issue. Body cameras were the old world's solution, but nowadays petabytes of footage cause too many cases of misconduct to go unseen, allowing wrongdoings to slip through. The result? A crisis of trust and legitimacy.

The solution

Veritas AI ingests large amounts of footage and, in near real time, flags potential misconduct such as excessive use of force, unlawful searches or discrimination and automatically compiles short, time‑stamped clips for review. Through advanced pattern recognition trained on verified incidents, it analyses conversation, movement and context to detect rule breaches as they happen.

Privacy by design

  • All civilian faces are automatically blurred on ingestion.
  • Nothing leaves the system until human‑verified.
  • Data minimisation and retention controls.

Human oversight

  • Every AI flag is reviewed by trained analysts before sharing.
  • Clear escalation paths and audit logs.
  • Calibration to local policies and law.

Benefits for everyone

  • Faster reviews and better training feedback for forces.
  • Fewer false complaints and clearer exonerations.
  • Transparent accountability for communities.
