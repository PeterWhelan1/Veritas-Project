What is Veritas AI?

Scenario (for survey context): In many countries, trust in policing has declined amid cases of misconduct and overwhelming volumes of unreviewed body‑cam footage. Veritas AI is a proposed system that ingests footage and flags potential misconduct in near real‑time (e.g., excessive force, unlawful search, discrimination), creating short, time‑stamped clips for human review. Civilians' faces are automatically blurred, and all AI flags are reviewed by a human before any action. The goal is accountability and faster learning feedback for police — not surveillance of the public.
